Challenge Your Memory!

Pair the images on the cards in this memory game. Fun brain training for both children and adults!

Instructions

On the game board, there are always two identical images. Start the game by flipping a card. Then try to find another card that has the same image as the first. If you can't find a pair, the flipped cards will be flipped back with the face down. Try to remember these images as it becomes easier to find pairs the longer you play. When you find a pair they are removed from the board. Match all pairs to finish the game!
